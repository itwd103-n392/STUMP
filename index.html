Week 1 Class by Pj and Jay ar

"Expanding knowloedge to hiphop."

Peter Jade Perez, also known to be called as PJ, is known to be a versatile dancer.He is a member of several solid crews in the Metro. From old school to new school, he has exposed himself into different style. He is a former member of A Team who has recently won at HHI 2014.

In his class, he has used several techniques and variations in dancing showcasing the technicality of the choreography. To see the choreography, watch the first video below.

Song title: Till the am - Troyboi

Jay ar Fernandez, also known to be called as Jay ar, specializes on the old school type of dances. He exudes his funky and groovy style added with a bit of technicality makes his style of dance unique from others. He has founded and the head of Built As Crew which is a dance crew in Adamson University.

In his class, he showed a technical, urban type of choreography. To see the choreography, watch the second video below.

Song Title: Good day - Tyga

Week 2 Class by RJ and Rem

"Advance techniques to hiphop."

Robert Johnson Bulacan , also known to called as RJ, is a member of FEU IAS Dance Company and is the head/choreographer until now. He specializes on technicality, and works more on your lower body moves. He usually dances urban choreography but is also versatile.

In his choreography, technique and technicality is eminent. To see the choreography, watch the first video below.

In his choreography, wide movements are eminent making it visible and powerful. To see the choreography, watch the second video below.

Week 3 Class by Jhavs and Dave

"Insane footworks and groovy dancehall moves."

Jhunvie De Paz , also known to be called as Jhavs, is a member of Artist Connection Exude Dancers. He specializes in urban choreography. He also does technical choreography tthat focuses on fast footworks. Jhavs always make sures he hit every singles beat he hears.

In his choreography, the technicality is eminent and insane which makes his craft unique and insane from the rest. To see the choreography, watch the first video below.

Song Title: sample

John Dave Isono, also known to be called as Dave , focuses on both on the choreography part and freestyling. His main genre is Femme. He also dances urban hiphop, house, old hip hop. He battled in several freestyle battle within the Metro. he makes Dancehall, Waacking choreography most of the time. Now, he is in process of experimenting. He incorporates several styles together to make his moves stands out.

In his choreography, techniques, variations, grooving is eminent. In this class, he teaches dancehall incorporating his own style. To see the choreography, watch the second video below.
